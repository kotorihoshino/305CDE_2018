Personal Data Privacy

Something you should remember when using social network


  • There is no real "delete" button once you post anything on the internet
  • In the future, You may not have the ability to control the posts today
  • Pictures and photos may also contain many personal data or information
  • Other people may collect your information without your permission and without you knowing
  • There is the possibility that being traced in the physical world through the information you posted on the internet
  • Other people may copy and posting your comment to other places without you knowing
  • Do not fully trust the auto delete function in the website
